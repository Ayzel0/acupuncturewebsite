ABOUT DOCTOR SU

Background

Dr. John Su is the founder and president of AcuTouch Acupuncture and Herb Center. He is also the Senior Professor and Clinic Supervisor at the nationally recognized Texas College of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Dr. Su earned his Doctor of Medicine Degrees from Heilongjiang University of Chinese Medicine, one of China's top medical schools. He had medical training under Dr. Sun Shen Tian, one of the top 10 acupuncture doctors in China. He is the author of 5 books on Chinese medicine and has published more than a dozen research papers in various professional journals. Dr. Su has received many awards and honors over the years. Dr. Su is reputably one of the leading and most skillful acupuncturists in practice, with more than 23 years of experience. He is particularly well known for his application of Acupuncture for the treatment of various diseases.
