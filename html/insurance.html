Insurance

We are a preferred in-network provider of most insurance companies. We will file insurance claims on your behalf if your insurance plan covers acupuncture. We will do all the paper work and also follow up to make sure your claim goes through.

It is the patient's responsibility to pay for your treatment before you meet your deductibles, and any denied claims for your treatment.
