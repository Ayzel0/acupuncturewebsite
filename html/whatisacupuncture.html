What is Acupuncture?

About Acupuncture

Overview

Acupuncture is an ancient Chinese medicine involving the insertion of fine needles into the body at specific points. These points have been mapped by Chinese doctors over a period of two thousand years, and recently electromagnetic research has confirmed the location of these points.
